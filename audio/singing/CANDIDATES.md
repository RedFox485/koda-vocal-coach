## STEM SEPARATION OPTION

If you find a CC-licensed full mix (with instruments) that has perfect verse/chorus vocal dynamics, you can isolate the vocals using Demucs (already installed):

```bash
demucs --two-stems vocals "input_song.wav"
# Output: separated/htdemucs/song_name/vocals.wav
```

Good sources for CC full-mix songs to separate:
- Free Music Archive: https://freemusicarchive.org/genre/Pop/
- Jamendo: https://www.jamendo.com/ (all CC-licensed)
- Loyalty Freak Music: https://loyaltyfreakmusic.com/

## EVALUATION CRITERIA FOR KODA DEMO

The ideal vocal stem should show:
1. **Green zone** (comfortable singing): Gentle verse sections, relaxed delivery
2. **Yellow/Red zone** (strain): Powerful chorus, belting, high notes, pushed delivery
3. **Clear transitions**: Obvious shift between easy and hard sections
4. **Pleasant quality**: Good singer, not amateur or rough
5. **Right length**: 1-2 minutes for demo video (can trim longer tracks)
